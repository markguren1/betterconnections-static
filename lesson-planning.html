Lesson Planning

Behind the curtain: one lesson, four engagement paths.

What this solves

  • Differentiation is time-consuming and often vague.
  • Students disengage for different reasons (by personality).

What BetterConnections gives you

  • One lesson plan with 4 “engagement hooks” (one per style)
  • Behavior supports aligned to each style
  • Fast prompts teachers can copy/paste

Example hook (short)

Driver: “Beat your best time.”   Connector: “Teach a partner.”   Analyst: “Find the pattern.”   Harmonizer: “Small-group support.”
