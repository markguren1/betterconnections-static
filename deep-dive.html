Deep Dive

Important Note
BetterConnections does not diagnose conditions. Personality styles apply to everyone. Some students may also have executive function challenges, ADHD, or anxiety — which can affect how personality shows up.

Support Lens

  • Executive Function: organization, initiation, follow-through
  • ADHD: attention regulation, impulse control
  • Anxiety: threat perception, emotional safety

These lenses help teachers adjust expectations and communication — not label students.
