Grading Feedback

Behind the curtain: comments that motivate the student in front of you.

What this solves

  • Generic comments don’t land the same for every student.
  • Teachers need fast feedback that’s firm + supportive.

What BetterConnections gives you

  • 3 comment styles (encouraging / direct / reflective)
  • Personality-aware wording
  • “Next step” phrasing that reduces shutdown

Example (short)

“You used strong evidence in paragraph two. Next time, add one sentence explaining why your evidence proves the claim.”
