Student Behavior

Behind the curtain: fast, personality-aware strategies that actually land.

What this solves

  • One “behavior strategy” doesn’t work for every student.
  • Teachers need a script they can use in 10 seconds.

What BetterConnections gives you

  • 2-minute plan for tomorrow
  • Do/Don’t list based on student style
  • In-the-moment scripts (calm, short, effective)

Example (script)

Teacher: “Here are two choices: A) sit here and start #1, or B) move to the focus seat and start #1. Which one are you choosing?”

Why it works: reduces power struggle and gives a clear next step.
