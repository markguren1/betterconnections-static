Hot Buttons

“Hot Buttons” are the quick triggers that make people feel respected… or disrespected. Use them to build faster connections and avoid unnecessary conflict.

How to use this section: Choose a personality type below. You’ll see what matters most, what to avoid, and quick “real life” examples a teacher can use immediately.
