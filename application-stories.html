Application Stories

Short, real-world scenarios showing how personality differences show up in work, leadership, interviews, and high-stakes conversations.

Cadence: Monthly — released monthly to preserve quality and practical reuse.

Month 1: Leadership Misalignment
I said the right thing… but it landed wrong.
4 stories
Goal: Show how mismatched styles between leaders and reports create friction — and how to adjust tone, structure, and pacing.
The Data Dump That Backfired
Types involved: Analytical leader → Expressive team member
Scenario: A long, detailed update overwhelms the recipient and triggers emotion instead of clarity.
Key takeaway: Lead with the headline and intent. Add details after connection.
The Too-Brief Direction
Types involved: Driver leader → Amiable report
Scenario: A short directive lands as cold or dismissive.
Key takeaway: Add one line of shared goal or support.
Month 2: Team Friction
They’re not being difficult — they’re being different.
4 stories
Goal: Reduce unnecessary conflict inside teams by matching communication style to personality style.
The Meeting That Went Sideways
Types involved: Driver facilitator → Expressive teammate
Scenario: Decisions move too fast; creative input feels shut down.
Key takeaway: Invite input before locking decisions.
Month 3: Interviews & High-Stakes Conversations
Same answer — different impact.
4 stories
Goal: Help professionals tailor tone and framing in interviews, negotiations, and performance conversations.
The Flat Answer
Types involved: Analytical candidate → Expressive interviewer
Scenario: A precise but emotionally flat answer loses impact.
Key takeaway: Add one human sentence to build connection.
